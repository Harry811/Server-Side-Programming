Welcome to IET-DAVV

Welcome to the Institute of Engineering and Technology (IET), Devi Ahilya Vishwavidyalaya (DAVV), a leading institution dedicated to engineering excellence. Since 1996, IET-DAVV has been a beacon of innovation and quality education in Central India, offering diverse undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering and technology.

Campus life at IET-DAVV is vibrant and inclusive, with a wide range of student clubs, organizations, and events. From technical clubs to cultural and sports activities, students have ample opportunities to engage, lead, and connect with their peers.

IET-DAVV values industry partnerships, providing students with internships and placement support to bridge the gap between academia and the professional world. Our strong connections with leading companies give students an edge in their future careers.

Explore our departments, meet our faculty, and discover why IET-DAVV is the right choice for your education.
