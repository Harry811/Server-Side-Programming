Bijasan Mata Temple

Bijasan Mata Temple is a revered Hindu temple located on a hilltop in Indore. The temple is dedicated to the goddess Bijasan, and it offers breathtaking views of the surrounding area.

Visitors can climb the steps to reach the temple and enjoy the spiritual atmosphere and scenic views.
