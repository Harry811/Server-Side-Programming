Krishna Pura Chhatri

Krishna Pura Chhatri is another set of beautiful cenotaphs located near the banks of the Khan River. These chhatris are dedicated to the Holkar rulers and are known for their stunning architectural design.

Visitors can enjoy a peaceful stroll through the area and take in the historic beauty of the cenotaphs.
