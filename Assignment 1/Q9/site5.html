Chhatri Bagh

Chhatri Bagh is a historical garden complex in Indore, known for its beautiful cenotaphs or "chhatris." These cenotaphs are dedicated to the Holkar rulers and are notable for their intricate carvings and detailed architecture.

Visitors can explore the garden and enjoy the serene atmosphere while learning about the history of the Holkars.
