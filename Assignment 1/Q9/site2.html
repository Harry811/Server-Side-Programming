Lalbagh Palace

Lalbagh Palace is a magnificent royal residence in Indore. It was built in three phases by the Holkar rulers, starting from the late 19th century. The palace features intricate European architecture and expansive gardens.

Visitors can tour the grand rooms and marvel at the exquisite interiors, which include chandeliers and fine art.
