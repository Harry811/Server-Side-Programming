Sarafa Bazaar

Sarafa Bazaar is Indore's vibrant night market, where you can find a wide array of street food and local delicacies. This bazaar is famous for its lively atmosphere and bustling crowds at night.

Visitors can enjoy street food, shop for local crafts, and experience the vibrant nightlife of Indore.
